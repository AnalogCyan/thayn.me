Self-Hosted Library

I host my personal music library on Valkyrie, my homelab server, using Navidrome.

Every album I care about lives there in full quality, properly tagged, with artwork and metadata exactly how I want it. No disappears because of licensing changes. Nothing gets edited or swapped out without me knowing.

I prefer owning my music instead of renting access to it. Streaming is convenient, but convenience is not the same thing as control. When you self host, your library is yours. It works offline. It does not get pulled. It does not change unless you decide to change it.

If that approach appeals to you, it is more approachable than it sounds. All you really need are your files, a small server or spare machine, and the right client apps to access your collection from anywhere.
